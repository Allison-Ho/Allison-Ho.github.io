ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS, LIZARD, SPOCK RULE

Rock, Paper, Scissors is played by player choosing a hand signal that the player would like to play. The computer will also randomly chooses one hand signal to play against the player. There is two version of this game, one is the original version, with only 3 hand signals; rock, paper, and scissors. The harder version is adapted from the game version played by Sheldon Cooper from the TV Show The Big Bang Theory. For each version, the outcome is determined by these simple rules:

Rock, Paper, Scissors

  • Rock wins against scissors
  • Scissors wins against paper
  • Paper wins against rock

Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock

  • Scissors wins against paper
  • Paper wins against rock
  • Rock wins against lizard
  • Lizard wins against Spock
  • Spock wins against scissors
  • Scissors wins against lizard
  • Lizard wins against paper
  • Paper wins against spock
  • Spock wins against rock
  • Rock wins against scissors
